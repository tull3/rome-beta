The Tull Project

This is my project, my blog, and my Java EE testing platform. The application generating these web pages is called "Rome." I named it that for what it will be in my life: a hub of productivity and growth. Eventually, all roads will lead back to Rome. My vision for this site is not only to show my abilities as an I. T. professional and test new software, but also to promote the culture and businesses of Charleston. I'd like to partner with locals to develop mutually-beneficial content and possibly even bring in other contributors - creative professionals who have a skill and a passion for it and this wonderful town. If you are a business or individual who wants to take part in what we are doing here or who has a digital need that I can help meet, then my contact information is at www.tull.digital.
